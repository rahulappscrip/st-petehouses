FAQ

Frequently asked questions about cash buyer reviews.

How fast can I get a cash offer in St Pete?

You can typically receive a no-obligation cash offer within 24–72 hours after your inquiry. Once you share basic property details, we review the information and present an offer quickly so you can make an informed decision.

Are there fees or commissions when selling to a cash buyer?

No commissions or agent fees are charged when selling to We Buy St Pete Houses. The offer you receive is the amount you'll get at closing, with no hidden costs.

What kinds of repairs are required for a cash offer?

Most cash buyers purchase as-is, so repairs are not required for an offer. Some sellers choose to make minor fixes, but we buy homes in any condition — no repairs necessary.

What if I have liens or other title issues?

Cash buyers can work with you if there are title issues. A title company can help clear liens and facilitate closing, and we'll guide you through the process to resolve any complications.

How do I compare cash offers vs listing with an agent?

Compare speed, certainty, and costs. Cash offers are typically faster and avoid commissions; agent listings may yield a higher price but take longer and involve more costs. The best choice depends on your timeline and priorities.

Is there a BBB rating for We Buy St Pete Houses?

We encourage you to check BBB ratings and other reviews when evaluating credibility. Trust signals like customer testimonials and verified contact details help you make an informed decision.

What is a cash home buyer in St Petersburg, FL?

A cash home buyer is an investor who pays with cash, often purchasing quickly and buying homes as-is. This eliminates financing risks and allows for a faster, simpler transaction.

What is the typical closing timeline for a cash sale in St Pete?

Cash closings can happen quickly — often within 7 days, depending on title work and logistics. You choose the closing date that works for your schedule.
